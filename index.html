Hello, I'm Adam Brummer. Driven by a commitment to excellence, I bring a blend of technical expertise and a customer-centric approach to my professional endeavors. I am eager to contribute to innovative projects and collaborate with like-minded professionals in the exciting field of technology.

My professional journey has evolved from customer service roles to account management, providing me with a well-rounded perspective in client relations.

In September 2021, I made a strategic move to Tulsa, OK, to pursue my passion for computer programming at Atlas School. This educational endeavor has been transformative, allowing me to delve into a multitude of languages and platforms such as C, C#, Unity, JavaScript, Python, SQL, and more. Notably, my academic journey has rekindled my interest in cybersecurity and internet safety. I aspire to leverage this passion professionally, with a future goal of working in a security-focused role within the dynamic realm of game development. I believe in the intersection of creativity and security, envisioning a role where I contribute to crafting secure and immersive gaming experiences.
